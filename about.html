Not a tour guide in the conventional sense.

We are readers of cities — trained to notice what most people walk past: the structural logic of a facade, the political subtext of a mural, the quiet stories embedded in a neighborhood.

Viacalli was born from years of guiding curious travelers through Mexico City’s most extraordinary spaces — and from the belief that meaningful experiences should be accessible to anyone willing to look deeper.

These tours are not itineraries with a fixed set of facts. They are conversations, shaped by who is in the group. Everyone brings a different perspective to the city — and that difference is part of what makes every tour unique.

Over the years, we have guided visitors from across the world — including architects, artists, students, journalists, and first-time travelers to Mexico City. Every tour becomes a different way of understanding the city, shaped by the people exploring it together.

Mexico City is one of the world's most layered and emotionally complex urban environments. We've spent years learning to read it. We'd like to share that reading with you.

🎨 Muralism

The Wall as Manifesto

Rivera, Orozco, Siqueiros — three artists, one radical proposition: that the walls of public buildings belong to everyone. We visit the SEP, the Palacio Nacional, and Bellas Artes to decode the grammar of revolution, identity, and myth painted across the walls of Mexico City's most extraordinary spaces.
